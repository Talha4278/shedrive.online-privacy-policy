Privacy Policy for shedrive.online

Effective Date: January 2025

SheDrive is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ride-sharing mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • CNIC (Computerized National Identity Card) number
  • License number (for drivers)
  • Vehicle information (for drivers)
  • Profile photographs

1.2 Location Information

  • Real-time GPS location during rides
  • Pickup and destination addresses
  • Route information

1.3 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

1.4 Payment Information

  • Payment method details (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Transaction history

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • To provide ride-sharing services
  • To match passengers with drivers
  • To process payments
  • To send ride confirmations and updates

2.2 Safety and Security

  • To verify user identities
  • To ensure driver qualifications
  • To provide emergency services
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

2.3 Communication

  • To send service notifications
  • To provide customer support
  • To send promotional offers (with consent)

2.4 Improvement

  • To analyze app performance
  • To improve user experience
  • To develop new features

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with:

  • Drivers (limited information for ride coordination)
  • Payment processors
  • Cloud storage providers (Supabase)
  • Analytics services

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger or acquisition, user information may be transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Expo: App development platform
  • Payment processors: For secure transactions

8. Location Services

We collect location information to:

  • Match you with nearby drivers
  • Provide real-time tracking
  • Calculate ride distances and fares
  • Ensure ride safety

You can control location permissions in your device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than Pakistan. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at:

  • Email: admin@shedrive.online

13. Consent

By using shedrive.online, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
